A Little About Me

Although I was born and raised in New England, I’ve been a resident of southern Nevada since 2004. As a military family, we lived in seven different states in 18 years. Once we settled in Henderson, we knew this was our forever home. My two sons graduated from local high schools before joining the United States Navy. I completed two undergraduate degrees at College of Southern Nevada. This is my home.

I absolutely love the Vegas valley and all it has to offer. Great hiking trails, amazing scenery, unlimited entertainment, exciting Vegas Golden Knights games… these are a few of my favorite things! No matter the season, there are always outdoor activities to jump into. Skiing and snowboarding on Mount Charleston, hiking in beautiful Red Rock Canyon, or swimming and boating on Lake Mead? No need to choose! You can do it all here.

My previous careers included stay-at-home mom, military wife, and legal assistant. Once I became a REALTOR®, I knew I’d found my true calling. I specialize in residential sales and rentals in Boulder City, Henderson, Las Vegas, and North Las Vegas. Looking in Pahrump or Mesquite? No problem! I can assist you in these areas either personally, or with our extensive network of agents.

The area has certainly changed in my time here, and I know it can be overwhelming to take in all the factors of a move.  Schools, services, dog parks, recreational options, commuter concerns - I’ve grown with the Valley and look forward to sharing my insights and expertise.
